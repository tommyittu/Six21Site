Why

Six21?

1
Life-changing opportunities. At Six21 all students will have access to opportunities that most sixth forms cannot provide. By working closely with our partners, every single student will be able to collaborate with academics and experts, they'll work on challenging projects with real employers, and they'll design and deliver a campaign that makes a difference to the community.
2
You are unique. At Six21, we'll treat you this way. Every student will have a bespoke programme of learning including A-level subjects, industry placements, project qualifications, and one-to-one careers mentoring from an industry expert. And if you still want support after you leave Six21, we'll be available to caoch and mentor you until you feel confident and on the right track.
3
World class teaching. School 21 has developed a reputation for high standards and cutting edge teaching and learning and we are rated 'outstanding' in all areas by Ofsted. Our experienced faculty of teachers have an exceptional track record of A-level teaching and supporting students into the best coursed and the best universities.

Who are

Six21?

You’ve done your GCSEs. You’ve worked hard. You’re ambitious. You want to make a difference to the world. You want to go to a good university but you have an eye on your future career as well. You don’t want a boring job but something filled with spark and excitement and the chance to grow in a variety of ways.

Six21 might be what you're looking for. We have fantastic teachers with experience of getting students into top universities. We also have extraordinary partners who work with us to give you life-changing experiences. Experiences you can't get anywhere else. A career in mediceine or science? A career in the creative industries? The chance to work with top creative talents in theatres, film production companies, high tech businesses.

Starting in September 2017, Six21 will be a pioneering new institution for the ambitious, the academic, and the enterprising.

Our aim is to give people the tools to make a difference to the world. Harnessing School 21's track record for developing exceptional students who are eloquent speakers, skilled problem solvers and creative thinkers, Six21 will develop not jus great academic qualifications but curious, critical thinkers who innovate with ease and who move with agility from one way of working to another. Our students will leave us ready to thrive in an increasingly complex and uncertain world - they will be masters of their own destiny.

School 21 has developed a reputation for high standards and cutting edge teaching and earning practices. Ofsted graded School 21 outstanding in all categories and commented that "Pupils across the school make exceptional progress" and that "pupils achieved remarkable standards of work and demonstrated knowledge and skills at levels beyond those expected for their age group".
